Frequently Asked FAQ's

01.

Why having Insurance is important?

Insurance provides financial protection against unexpected events such as :

  • illness
  • accidents
  • theft
  • Natural Disasters
  • Insurance also offers peace of mind, knowing you’re safeguarded against risks. It’s essential for managing uncertainty and planning for the future. Additionally, many types of insurance, like car and health insurance, are legally required or necessary for compliance with contracts. Ultimately, insurance helps ensure stability and security, allowing people to focus on life without the constant worry of potential financial setbacks.

02.

What is a General Insurance?

General insurance refers to any insurance policy that covers non-life assets, providing protection against financial losses due to a wide range of unforeseen events. It contrasts with life insurance, which deals with the insurance of life or health. General insurance policies are typically designed to protect property, vehicles, health, travel, and business.

What are the different types of general insurance in our company?

  • Property Insurance: Protects businesses or individuals against losses or damages to buildings, equipment, or other property.
  • Motor Insurance: Covers vehicles against accidents, theft, or damage. It includes two types: Third-party liability and Comprehensive insurance.
  • Marine general insurance : provides coverage for ships, cargo, and other maritime-related activities. It protects against risks such as damage to vessels, cargo loss, theft, accidents during transit, and liability claims. This type of insurance is essential for businesses involved in shipping and transportation, ensuring financial protection for goods and assets while they are being transported over water.
  • Personal accident general insurance provides financial protection in the event of accidental injury, disability, or death. It typically covers medical expenses, hospitalization costs, and compensation for permanent disabilities or loss of income due to an accident. Unlike health insurance, which covers illnesses, personal accident insurance specifically focuses on accidents, ensuring immediate support for you or your family during unexpected situations.

03.

What is the difference between general insurance and life insurance?

General insurance covers non-life risks and provides coverage for specific incidents, while life insurance provides financial support in the event of death or serious illness. Below is the purpose :

  • General Insurance: Covers non-life risks such as property damage, liability, health, and accidents. It provides financial protection against specific events like car accidents, home damage, or medical expenses.
  • Life Insurance: Provides financial support to beneficiaries in the event of the policyholder's death or serious illness. It is designed to offer financial security to dependents or cover long-term financial needs.
